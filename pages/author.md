Tom Rodriguez built a decades-long career in software and cloud security — picking up a technical credit on the 1998 film *Godzilla* along the way — before turning his attention to fiction. A lifelong sailor and a long-lapsed private pilot, he's drawn to stories about hope held onto under the worst possible circumstances. He once, during flight training, managed to shut down all the air traffic over Burbank Airport — a story he is now content to tell from the deck of a sailboat. He lives in Southern California.
