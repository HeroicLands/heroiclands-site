The pages below are the reference catalogs behind [Song of Heroic Lands](/project/sohl/) and [HârnMaster 3](/project/harnmaster/). Every item, skill, trait, and ability that appears on a character sheet or creature stat block is defined in one of these catalogs.

# Domains

Divine and arcane domains — the thematic spheres that mysteries, invocations, and divine magic draw on.

- [Domains](/types/domain/)

# Characteristics

The intrinsic qualities of characters and creatures: what they can *do*, what they *are*, and what they *suffer*.

- [Skills](/types/skill/) — learned capabilities with trainable scores.
- [Traits](/types/trait/) — innate physical and mental qualities.
- [Mysteries](/types/mystery/) — religious and philosophical mysteries available to clerics, philosophers, and initiates.
- [Mystical Abilities](/types/mysticalability/) — arcane talents, invocations, and other supernatural capabilities.
- [Afflictions](/types/affliction/) — injuries, illnesses, curses, and other lasting adverse conditions.

# Possessions

Everything a character might carry, wield, wear, drink, or store.

- [Armor](/types/armorgear/) — head, torso, and limb protection across all materials.
- [Weapons](/types/weapongear/) — melee, thrown, and missile armaments.
- [Projectiles](/types/projectilegear/) — arrows, bolts, sling stones, and other ammunition.
- [Miscellaneous Gear](/types/miscgear/) — tools, rations, light sources, climbing equipment, and the rest of the adventuring miscellany.
- [Containers](/types/containergear/) — packs, pouches, bags, and waterskins.
- [Concoctions](/types/concoctiongear/) — potions, poisons, alchemical preparations, and ritual reagents.
